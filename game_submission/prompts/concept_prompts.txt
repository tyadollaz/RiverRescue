# Brainstorming prompts for game concept development
1. What unique mechanics can we introduce to enhance gameplay?
2. How can we incorporate environmental themes into the game narrative?
3. What character archetypes would resonate with our target audience?
4. What challenges can players face that reflect real-world issues?
5. How can we create a compelling backstory that engages players?
6. What visual styles can we explore to make the game stand out?
7. How can we integrate educational elements into the gameplay?
8. What emotions do we want players to experience while playing?
9. How can we encourage teamwork or competition among players?
10. What innovative features can we implement to keep players engaged?
